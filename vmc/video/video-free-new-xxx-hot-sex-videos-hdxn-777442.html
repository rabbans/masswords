Imsha Rehman Viral Leaked MMS Video Scandal: All About Pakistani Influencer, Her Net Worth, Family And More

Imsha Rehman Viral MMS Video Leaked: Pakistani influencer Imsha Rehman is facing controversy after an intimate video leaked online without her consent. The video spread rapidly across social media and WhatsApp, sparking criticism and backlash.

Imsha Rehman Viral MMS Video Leaked: Pakistani social media influencer Imsha Rehman has recently found herself at the centre of a distressing controversy, following the unauthorised leak of an intimate video online. This unfortunate incident bears an unsettling resemblance to the ordeal faced by fellow influencer Minahil Malik. The leaked MMS video of Imsha Rehman rapidly spread across various social media platforms, including WhatsApp, prompting widespread criticism and backlash. 

In response to the situation, Imsha Rehman took swift action by deactivating her TikTok account, while concurrently addressing the issue in her Instagram bio, stating, "Jab tak video viral hai maine ne ID off kar di hai" ("As long as the video is viral, I have turned off my ID"). 

Notably, her Instagram account remains active, with Imsha even sharing a video mere hours ago. As attention surrounds this young Pakistani influencer, curiosity arises regarding her background and personal life. 

Who is Imsha Rehman?

Imsha Rehman, a vibrant social media sensation, has captivated audiences in Pakistan with her lively and captivating content. Beyond her massive TikTok following, she maintains a strong presence across various social media platforms. Born on October 7, 2002, in Lahore, Pakistan, Rehman has shared glimpses of her life through interviews with local channels. 

Having grown up in Lahore, this young influencer has leveraged her charming personality and creative flair to build a formidable online reputation, solidifying her position as a beloved and influential figure among Pakistani youth.
